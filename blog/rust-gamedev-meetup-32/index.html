Rust Gamedev Meetup 32

The 32nd Rust Gamedev Meetup took place on November 11th. You can watch the recording of the meetup here on YouTube. The meetup took place on the Rust Gamedev YouTube and Twitch. The meetups take place on the second Saturday of every month via the Rust Gamedev Discord server and are also streamed on Twitch.
